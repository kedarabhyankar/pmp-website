Purdue Music Producers





We are Purdue Music Producers - we're all students at Purdue University, studying different majors,
but united out of our interest and love for making music. We meet every week in PAO B185, on Thursdays from 6:30PM to 8:30PM.

We all come from different music production backgrounds. Some of us like to make beats in a variety of programs - FL Studio, Ableton, Reaper, Pro Tools, and Logic Pro X, just to name a few. In addition, members who don't make beats may rap, sing, or just play an instrument - and they're all welcome. Even if the thought has ever crossed your mind of producing music, we highly urge you to come to the club. Boiler Up!
